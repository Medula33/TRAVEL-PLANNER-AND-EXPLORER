Explore Goa

Goa is a state on the western coast of India with coastlines stretching along the Arabian Sea. It’s known for its beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood.

  • Best time to visit: November to February
  • Popular beaches: Baga, Anjuna, Calangute
  • Local food: Goan fish curry, Bebinca
