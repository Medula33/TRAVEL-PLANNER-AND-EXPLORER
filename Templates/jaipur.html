Explore Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is the capital of Rajasthan. It’s famous for its royal heritage, palaces, forts, and vibrant culture.

  • Best time to visit: October to March
  • Main attractions: Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Amer Fort
  • Local food: Dal Baati Churma, Ghewar
