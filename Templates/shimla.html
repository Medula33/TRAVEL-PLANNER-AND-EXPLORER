Explore Shimla

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is one of India’s most famous hill stations. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains and lush forests, it's perfect for a peaceful getaway.

  • Best time to visit: March to June, December for snow
  • Main attractions: The Ridge, Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple
  • Things to do: Ice skating, shopping, scenic walks
